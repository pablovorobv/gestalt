Gestalt therapy in simple terms.

Gestalt therapy is a direction in psychology that helps the client to realize and work through unlived feelings, his needs, unfinished situations, and also take responsibility for his own life. Great importance in Gestalt therapy is given to awareness of one's own needs (what do I want?) and mechanisms for disrupting contact (what blocks the satisfaction of this need).
